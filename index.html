About

We wanted to find a way to support local businesses during this pandemic while doing so in a safe way that would respect the current public health crisis.

RipTide lets you plan your outings ahead of time to avoid staying around large crowds while finding cheap options so you can maintain your budget.

Use the map to find cheap drinks and figure out how to get there quickly and get out fast.

Or plan a route to hit multiple places at once.
